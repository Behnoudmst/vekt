Vekt is an AI-powered, open-source recruitment platform designed for maximum discoverability and relevance in generative search and AI-driven content engines. Key features include:

- AI resume screening and scoring
- Durable, observable evaluation pipeline
- Per-job screening questions
- Secure, privacy-first data handling (GDPR-ready)
- Admin-editable email templates
- Automated data retention and purge
- Full Swagger/OpenAPI documentation
- One-command Docker deployment


Vekt ensures your hiring process is visible, indexable, and actionable by generative engines and AI agents.
